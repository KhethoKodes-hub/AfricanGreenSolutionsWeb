Who We Are

Turning Waste
into Power.

African Green Solutions is a pioneering renewable energy company specializing in the manufacturing and installation of anaerobic digestors for biogas production, based in Vaal, Gauteng.

We are a proudly South African company committed to creating a cleaner and greener future. Every project we deliver transforms organic waste into clean energy — addressing Africa's growing demand for sustainable power while solving critical waste management challenges.

What Is Biogas?

Produced through anaerobic digestion of organic matter — food waste, agricultural waste, animal manure — in oxygen-free conditions. Microorganisms break down waste inside a sealed digester, releasing methane-rich gas harnessed for heating, cooking, and electricity generation.
