About Us

African Green Solutions is a pioneering renewable energy company specializing in the manufacturing and installation of anaerobic digestors for biogas production.

Based in Vaal, Gauteng, we provide environmentally friendly, long-term solutions for managing organic waste while addressing Africa's growing demand for sustainable energy.

We are a proudly South African, limited liability company committed to creating a cleaner and greener future through innovative biogas technology.

What Is Biogas?

Biogas is a renewable fuel produced through the anaerobic digestion of organic matter (such as food waste, agricultural waste, and animal manure) in oxygen-free conditions.

Microorganisms break down this waste inside a sealed anaerobic digester, releasing a mixture of gases—mainly methane, which can be harnessed for heating, cooking, and electricity.
