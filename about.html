Our story

From Vision to Green Revolution

African Green Solutions is a pioneering renewable energy company specializing in the manufacturing and installation of anaerobic digestors for biogas production. Founded with a vision to transform Africa’s energy landscape, we provide environmentally friendly solutions for organic waste while addressing the continent’s energy demand.

Based in Vaal, Gauteng, we are a proudly South African company dedicated to creating a cleaner, greener future through innovative biogas technology. Our expertise blends renewable energy, engineering, and environmental management to deliver cutting-edge systems that benefit both planet and people.

“Fuel the planet with what it feeds on — turning yesterday’s waste into tomorrow’s energy.”

Anaerobic digestion

Power of Biogas Technology

At the heart of our operations lies anaerobic digestion — a natural process where microorganisms break down organic matter in oxygen-free conditions. The result is biogas (methane-rich) for heating, cooking, and electricity, plus nutrient-rich organic fertilizer.
