About African Green Solutions

Our Story

African Green Solutions is a pioneering renewable energy company specializing in the manufacturing and installation of anaerobic digestors for biogas production. Founded with a vision to transform Africa's energy landscape, we are committed to providing environmentally friendly, long-term solutions for managing organic waste while addressing the continent's growing demand for sustainable energy.

Based in Vaal, Gauteng, we are a proudly South African, limited liability company dedicated to creating a cleaner and greener future through innovative biogas technology. Our team brings together expertise in renewable energy, engineering, and environmental management to deliver cutting-edge solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Our Technology

At the heart of our operations is anaerobic digestion technology - a natural process where microorganisms break down organic matter in the absence of oxygen. This process produces biogas, a renewable energy source primarily composed of methane, which can be used for heating, cooking, and electricity generation.

Our custom-designed anaerobic digestors are engineered to efficiently process various organic waste streams, including agricultural waste, food waste, and animal manure. The by-product of this process is a nutrient-rich organic fertilizer that completes the cycle of sustainability.

The Biogas Process

Waste Collection

Organic waste materials are collected from various sources including farms, food processing facilities, and households.

Anaerobic Digestion

Waste is processed in our sealed digestors where microorganisms break it down in oxygen-free conditions.

Biogas Production

The digestion process releases biogas, primarily methane, which is captured and stored for energy use.

Energy Generation

Biogas is used for heating, cooking, or converted to electricity through generators.

Fertilizer Production

The remaining digestate is processed into high-quality organic fertilizer for agricultural use.
