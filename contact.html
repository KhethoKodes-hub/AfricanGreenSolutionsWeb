Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to install a biogas system? +

The installation timeline varies depending on the scale and complexity of the system. Small-scale residential systems can be installed in 1-2 weeks, while larger commercial or agricultural systems may take 4-8 weeks from initial assessment to full commissioning.

What type of waste can be used in your biogas systems? +

Our systems can process various organic waste streams including agricultural waste (crop residues, animal manure), food waste, garden waste, and certain industrial organic by-products. During our feasibility study, we'll analyze your specific waste streams to determine the optimal solution.

Do you offer maintenance contracts? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance contracts tailored to your specific system and usage patterns. Our maintenance plans include regular inspections, performance monitoring, and emergency support to ensure your biogas system operates at peak efficiency.

What is the typical return on investment for a biogas system? +

ROI varies based on factors like system size, waste availability, energy costs, and potential revenue from fertilizer by-products. Most commercial systems achieve payback within 3-5 years. We provide detailed financial analysis during our feasibility studies to help you understand the specific ROI for your project.

Do you work with residential clients or only commercial? +

We serve both residential and commercial clients. Our solutions range from small-scale household systems to large industrial facilities. We'll design a system that matches your specific needs, whether you're a homeowner looking to reduce energy costs or a business seeking sustainable waste management solutions.
