Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?+
We respond within 24 hours on business days. For emergencies, our 24/7 line ensures a reply within 2 hours.
Do you offer free consultations?+
Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations and site assessments for qualified projects in Gauteng and surrounding provinces.
What info should I prepare before contacting?+
Type and quantity of organic waste, current energy consumption, available space, and project goals. This helps us give accurate advice.
What is your service area?+
We primarily serve Gauteng, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, and North West. Larger projects may be considered nationwide — contact us to discuss.
Do you work with residential clients?+
Absolutely! We provide solutions for households, farms, businesses, and municipalities — tailored to any scale.
